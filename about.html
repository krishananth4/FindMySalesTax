How Find My Sales Tax Helps You

The purpose of this application is to provide an easy and accessible way to find the sales tax of whatever location that you are interested in. We use an interactive map that will allow a user to click on a particular location and display the corresponiding sales tax. The motivation behind this project was to develop an application that has all the sales tax data rounded up in in a singular page with all the data readily available. One of our priorities was to provide sales tax information to the user in any georgaphical region/county in the United States through the use of an simple map.
